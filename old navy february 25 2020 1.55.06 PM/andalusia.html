ANDALUSIA SPAIN CULINARIA

NOVEMBER 12, 2017 TO NOVEMBER 19, 2017

GRANADA & SEVILLA, SPAIN/

WHY TRAVEL WITH OLDWAYS? In November 2017, Oldways, chef Deborah Hansen (left), and Ronni Baer, Senior Curator of European Paintings (right), led an Oldways culinaria to Granada and Sevilla, two of Andalusia Spain’s most vibrant and historic cities. Andalusia is known as a region of olive oil, sherry, ﬂamenco, festivals, tapas, and incredible historic and cultural landmarks. Based in the heart of both Granada and Sevilla, close to the major cathedrals in both cities, we made the most of the history and art of each place—learning how they shaped the culinary and cultural highlights of the region and these two iconic cities.

Exploration of Granada and Sevilla included:

Sunday, November 12, 2017

Check-in at the Hotel Palacio de Santa Paula in Granada

Welcome session along with guided tapas & wine tasting with Chef Deborah Hansen, followed by dinner in the chapel of the 16th-century convent at the Hotel Palacio Santa Paula

Monday, November 13, 2017

Guided tour of the Alhambra, followed by a traditional lunch at the Parador de Granada

Afternoon siesta

Evening tapas tour in Granada — divided into smaller groups, each with a tapas guide

Tuesday, November 14, 2017

Visit to the Mercado de San Agustin and guided tour of Granada’s Cathedral, featuring Ronni Baer’s art tour of the Royal Chapel of Granada

Lunch on own

Early evening cooking demo with Chef Deborah Hansen and Spanish chefs, followed by dinner

Wednesday, November 15, 2017

Check out of hotel and visit to a goat cheese farm and olive oil producer in Granada, followed by a light lunch

Travel by bus to Sevilla and check in at the Hotel Las Casas de la Juderia

Flamenco evening with tapas before and after

Thursday, November 16, 2017

Guided cultural tour of Sevilla’s Cathedral and Alcazar, followed by Ronni Baer’s lecture on Velazquez, Murillo, and Zurbaran in the Museo de Bellas Artes

Free lunch

Carriage ride to cooking class at a restaurant in the Maria Luisa Jardin

Hands-on cooking class and dinner — everyone cooks!>

Friday, November 17, 2017

Visit to an organic olive oil producer Alcubilla at Castro del Rio and wine producer Alvear at Montilla

Lunch in the wine cellar

Free evening in Sevilla
