CYPRUS CULINARY TOUR 2019

NOVEMBER 10, 2019 TO NOVEMBER 17, 2019/p>

NOTE: THIS TRIP HAS ALREADY TAKEN PLACE.

Travellers were immersed into the heart of Mediterranean lifestyle and cuisine on this 7-day foodie’s tour of Cyprus! Called
Aphrodite’s Island, Cyprus oﬀers travelers beautiful beaches, ancient historical sites, seaside tavernas, and plenty of delicious
culinary traditions.

Chefs Ana Sortun and Cassie Piumajoined Oldways and Aglaia Kremezi in leading the culinary and cultural exploration.

The Cyprus Culinary Tour Included:

7 nights hotel accomodation

Fabulous meals, plus cooking classes with chefs Ana, Cassie & Aglaia

Visits to producers of halloumi, wine, bread, and spoon sweets

Historical, artistic, and cultural visits to museums, monasteries, markets, castles, and much more

CPEU credits were available for RDs.
