SICILY CULINARIA 2018

APRIL 08, 2018 TO APRIL 15, 2018

PALERMO, AGRIGENTO, AND SIRACUSA, ITALY

WHY TRAVEL WITH OLDWAYS? On this memorable trip, Oldways and world-renowned nutrition and lifestyle medicine expert Dr. David Katz and his wife, Dr. Catherine Katz, a scientist and Mediterranean cooking expert, joined forces for a week-long exploration of the dazzling island of Sicily. Read on for an overview of the trip, and check out Catherine Katz’s website to see the group in action!

We spent four nights in Palermo and three in Siracusa (Ortigia), with lots of adventure in between. In Palermo, we visited spectacular food markets and museums, and enjoyed a longer guided tour of the Monreale Cathedral and its magniﬁcent mosaics. We had a day at Anna Tasca Lanza’s legendary cooking school/winery/restaurant/hotel Regaleali, and another day at Agrigento, a large archaeological site with well-preserved Greek temples. We ﬁnished our week in and around Ortigia, a small island that is the historical center of the city of Siracusa, well known for its stunning Greek amphitheater built in the 5th century BC.

Registered dietitians were eligible for an estimated 17 CPE credits.

Our Sicilian exploration included:

Sunday, April 8 — Benvenuto

Arrival in Palermo and check in at Grand Hotel Wagner

Welcome Session with a presentation by Dr. David Katz, followed by a Sicilian Wine Tasting and Welcome Dinner

Monday, April 9 — Tradition

Guided tour of Palermo, with a walk through the city’s Capo Market and a visit to the spectacular baroque cathedral.

Lunch in Palermo at Corona Trattoria, with a presentation by culinary historian Mary Taylor Simeti

Siesta

Dinner in Palermo

Tuesday, April 10 — Regaleali Day

Arrive at Regaleali, a cooking school/winery/restaurant

Wine tour and visit to the gardens

Discussion with David and Catherine Katz about Mediterranean cooking—Sicilian style

Cooking demo and wine tasting

Lunch: same foods as cooking demo

Return to Palermo and free evening in Palermo

Wednesday, April 11 — Art and Culture

Palermo Street Food Tour (divided into small groups)

Guided visit to the Monreale Cathedral with its magniﬁcent mosaics

Early Dinner at Cucina Papoﬀ

Thursday, April 12 — Agrigento: Valley of the Gods, Valley of the Temples

Check out of the hotel and depart for Agrigento

Guided tour of Agrigento, a large Greek archaeological site

Country lunch with an olive oil tasting by Giuseppe Taibi, of Oilio Taibi

Drive to Siracusa and check in at the Grand Hotel Ortigia

Free evening in Ortigia or Siracusa

Friday, April 13 — Cooking!

Cooking Class with Catherine and David Katz

Lunch: the dishes cooked in the class

Free time

Dinner in Ortigia

Saturday, April 14 — Options!

Choose between

Vineyard tour and lunch OR

Guided visit to Modica (for chocolate) and Ragusa (for cheese) OR

Guided tour of ancient Siracusa OR

Free day to shop or relax

Farewell dinner in Ortigia

Sunday, April 15 — Arrivederci

Departure from Catania airport or additional travel on own
